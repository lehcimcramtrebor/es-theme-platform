Here you can place your own logo. Can be named "logo-fr.png", "logo-fr.svg" or  "logo-us.png", "logo-us.svg" etc.. for custom collections logo for example.

Just name it

	-"logo.png" or "logo.svg" for default region (EUR)

	-"logo_us.png" or "logo_us.svg" for US region (US)

	-"logo_jp.png" or "logo_jp.svg" for JP region (JP)
	
You can put all region files in same time, emulation station will select the good logo with your region settings.
